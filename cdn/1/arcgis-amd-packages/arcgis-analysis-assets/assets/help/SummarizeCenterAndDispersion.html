The Summarize Center and Dispersion tool finds the central feature, central tendency, and standard deviational ellipse of a set of features.

The point, line, or polygon features for which the central feature, central tendency, and standard deviational ellipse will be calculated.

Specifies the methods that will summarize the center and dispersion of the features.

Specifies the size, in standard deviations, of the elliptical polygon. If distance between the mean center and feature locations follows a Rayleigh distribution, the 1 standard deviation, 2 standard deviations, and 3 standard deviations option will output an elliptical polygon that will cover 63 percent, 89 percent, and 99.9 percent of the features, respectively.

The field that will be used to determine the weight of the feature.

The field that will be used to group features together. The selected Summary types will be calculated for each group.

The name of the layer that will be created and added to the map.

The name of the folder in My Content where the result will be saved.
