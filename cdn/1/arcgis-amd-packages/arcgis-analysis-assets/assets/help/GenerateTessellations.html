The Generate Tessellations tool creates a grid of polygons based on the specified extent, shape, and size.

The shape of the tessellations that will be created.

Specifies whether the tessellation size will be defined by its area (Area) or by a distance value (Distance).

The size (area or distance) of the generated tessellations. For squares, hexagons, triangles, and diamonds, the distance determines the height of the tessellations. For transverse hexagons, the distance determines the width of the tessellations.

The H3 resolution of the tessellations. Resolution ranges from 0 to 15. With each increasing resolution size, the area of the polygons will decrease to one seventh the size at the lower resolution.

Specifies the units that will be used for the tessellation size.

The extent that the tessellations will cover.

For feature layers, specifies whether the tessellations will cover the entire extent or only cover the areas where the tessellations intersect with features.

The name of the layer that will be created and added to the map.

The name of the folder in My Content where the result will be saved.
